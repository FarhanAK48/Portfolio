YoderLay

This is Ionic application which name is Yoderlay, designed to provide a seamless online service for individuals facing issues with everyday essentials such as electrical systems, ovens, refrigerators, plumbing, and more. The application enables users to report faults or request assistance, and it assigns the task to a nearby mechanic. Additionally, users can apply for services through the app, with oversight and management provided by the admin.
